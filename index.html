About Us

The IoT Research Lab is a multidisciplinary hub where innovation meets technology. Established to foster collaboration among students, researchers, and industry partners, our mission is to create and deploy IoT solutions that enhance sustainability and improve quality of life. We provide research facilities, mentoring, and open projects that encourage hands-on experimentation and problem-solving.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To become a leading center of excellence in IoT research and innovation, empowering communities through smart and connected technologies.

Mission

  • Develop sustainable IoT-based solutions for real-world challenges.
  • Promote collaboration between academia, industry, and community.
  • Equip students with practical IoT research and development skills.
  • Encourage ethical and responsible use of connected technologies.
